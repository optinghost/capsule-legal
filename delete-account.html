You can delete your Capsule account and all associated data directly from within the app. No email or form submission is required — deletion is immediate and permanent.

How to Delete Your Account

Follow these steps inside the Capsule app:

  1. Open the Capsule app and go to the Settings tab (bottom navigation bar).
  2. Scroll down to the Account section.
  3. Tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the deletion in the dialog that appears.
Warning: This action is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be deleted immediately.

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your Firebase Authentication account
  • All journal entries (dates, moods, written thoughts)
  • All context card data (emotions, weather, people, meals, health, hobbies, chores)
  • All photos attached to your entries (from Firebase Storage)
  • Your account document and card configuration from Firestore

What Is Not Retained

There is no data retention period. All data listed above is deleted immediately upon account deletion. We do not keep backups of individual user data after deletion.

Local Data

After deleting your account, any locally cached data remaining on your device (stored in the app's private storage) will be cleared the next time the app is opened. You can also clear it immediately by uninstalling the app.

Advertising Data

Advertising identifiers and usage data collected by Google AdMob are managed by Google and are not deleted by us. You can manage your ad preferences and data through Google's data controls.
